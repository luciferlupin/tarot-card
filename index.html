Three Card Reading

The three-card reading is one of the most popular and insightful tarot spreads. Each card position represents a different aspect of your journey:

  • Past: Reflects past influences and experiences that have shaped your current situation
  • Present: Represents your current energy and circumstances
  • Future: Offers guidance and potential outcomes based on your current path
